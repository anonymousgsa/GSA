GSA is the first to tackle category-level registration in 3D Gaussian Splatting, leveraging geometry-aware features and a two-step optimization to enable cross-instance alignment.



Synchronized Novel View Synthesis

Our registration method enables the alignment of different 3DGS models within the same category. This approach is effective for both synthetic and real scenes, unlocking new possibilities such as synchronized novel view synthesis and enabling multi-view consistent object replacement.
